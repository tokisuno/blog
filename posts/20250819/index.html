Toki Talks - Obscure YouTubers I Remember

2025/08/19

Welcome!

Welcome back to another week of Toki Talks! This week, I just want to shout out some old usernames of people I used to know/watch as a child. I hope that this page can be indexed online one day and I can either get in contact with some of lesser-known guys from my childhood, or maybe talk to some people who also watched these growing up :) This isn’t an extensive list by any means, as I know there are a lot of people from art communities I used to be a part of that were either exposed, have changed names, have changed genders, and don’t want to be mentioned most likely to put their name to rest.

The lists

Lego Customs

But these examples are like, the tip top of the people that inspired me to make content. Along the way, I have been in and out of many communities. The first real online community I was a part of was the Lego Customs community of the early 2010s. Prominent members of that time included:

  • MrOrangeLego
  • GabbaGabbaStudios
  • TehSupahSmasher (EpicLegoCustoms528) <– my first online friend
  • SonicSimon

The community and scene has grown a lot since then, and basically no one here makes content anymore. It’s of an era long passed, but I am sure there after me writing this post, I’m going to receive a message one day from someone who was also deep-diving to find these people and say “oh wow, I can’t believe I found someone else who watched them!”

Let’s Plays!

I used to love watching LPs as a kid, a lot. But when looking back at some of the creators I used to watch, some of them have just faded with time and were never actually super big creators like I remembered. Just remember that the names I am mentioning here are people I watched in 2012-2013, and I haven’t watched their videos in over a decade. So if anyone goes and watches a video, and hears something they don’t like, don’t get mad at me LMAO.

  • Yohhamgambal
  • PlayingWithMahWii

I was obviously also really into Chuggaaconroy and remember fondly watching TheRunawayGuys’ LP of New Super Mario Bros Wii and Smash 64. I think it’s because I really enjoyed watching other people having fun with their friends, as I couldn’t really do that for the longest time with gaming. I was an only child, and even when playing online with friends it’s just not the same. There’s something special about gaming in person with people you know and love.
