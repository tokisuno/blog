Stimulation

2023/05/29

one’s quest for stimluation

As someone who has ADHD, finding stimulation is a struggle. I find myself having urges I wish I didn’t had to gain some sort of happiness inside me, mainly temporary. The internet ruined my dopamine receptors and it’s something I am actively trying to fix. I find myself latching onto anything that brings me joy for the short amount of time that it does. Let’s call this “being in my own world”. When I am in this “world” of mine, for however long it may be, I find myself disconnected from reality trying to get into some sort of groove or zone. Anyone that tries to break me out of it will see a not very pleasant side of me. Finding anything to stimulate my brain is difficult. When I do find it, I don’t want to get out of it. It’s difficult to break out of it, and to try and rationalize inside of my head that this isn’t normal. Adults don’t act like teenagers in front of their parents because they want to try and get “one more game in”. That’s not how it works. That’s being a glorified man-child, and I’ve found myself becoming than more and more as time goes on without fixing the root of the problem, being my relation with what stims me.
