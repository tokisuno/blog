## preface 
For about 19 years of my life, I was undiagnosed with ADHD. Since then, I have taken strides in figuring myself out, what I want in life, and the best methods of doing so. Something I've always been interested in is reading. I always found reading to be an escape, but I couldn't do it for many years since I could never focus on a book for more than 5 minutes at a time at most. My brain was filled with excess thoughts, which is from a combination of OCD and ADHD both being unmedicated for so long. I am now 21 years old, medicated, and love reading. I have been using a Kindle since I first started taking medication, and I have since moved on from it to a Moan Inkpalm 5 I got for my birthday off of aliexpress. I haven't looked back since. These are my thoughts.
